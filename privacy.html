2. Personal Data We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following categories of data:

  • Identity Data: A unique account ID (UUID) generated by the App, your chosen username, and unique identifiers (sub) provided by OAuth providers (Apple or Google).
  • Contact Data (Optional): Your email address and verification status, only if provided by Apple or Google upon your authorization during sign-in.
  • Usage and Gameplay Data: Match history, win rates, role/faction statistics, in-game token usage, matchmaking (lobby) information, friend lists, and in-app purchase history.
  • Device and Diagnostic Data: Crash reports (stack traces), device, and OS information.
  • Identifiers: Mobile advertising IDs (IDFA for iOS, AAID for Android).

We do not request or collect access to your camera, microphone, precise location, contacts, photos/videos, or audio recordings. There is no in-app chat system.

3. Purposes of Processing and Legal Basis

Your personal data is processed in accordance with KVKK Art. 5/6 and GDPR Art. 6 for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision (Performance of a Contract): To create accounts, manage lobbies, save progress, and maintain the token economy.
  • Legal Obligations: To verify in-app purchases and respond to legal requests if required.
  • Legitimate Interests: To ensure app stability via error tracking (Sentry) and performance telemetry (OpenTelemetry), and to prevent fraud and cheating.
  • Consent: For showing personalized ads (via the App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS).

4. Third-Party Sharing and International Transfers

While the Data Controller is located in Turkey, our API servers are located in Germany (Hetzner Cloud - EU). Your data may be shared and processed internationally by the following third-party service providers to facilitate the App:

  • Apple and Google: For authentication (OAuth) and in-app purchase (IAP) receipt validation.
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions and IAPs (associated with your Account ID).
  • Google AdMob: For delivering rewarded ads and Server-Side Verification (SSV) of ad rewards.
  • Sentry: For crash and error reporting.

5. Data Retention and Deletion (Account Deletion)

  • Ephemeral Data: Lobby and active match states are stored temporarily and are automatically deleted within 24 hours at the latest.
  • Persistent Data: Kept as long as your account is active.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time within the App (Profile → Delete Account). This is a hard delete action. Your account, friendships, invites, and sessions will be immediately erased. Your match history and statistics will be permanently anonymized (Account ID removed).

6. Ads and App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

The App features only user-initiated rewarded ads.

  • For iOS Users: We implement the App Tracking Transparency framework. We will ask for your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for personalized advertising. If you decline, you will still receive ads, but they will not be personalized.
  • Ad rewards are securely verified on our servers using your Account ID.

7. User Rights (KVKK and GDPR)

Under the KVKK (Article 11) and GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data (right to be forgotten), restrict processing, request data portability, and withdraw your consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at alperreren.apps@gmail.com. EU users have the right to lodge a complaint with a local supervisory authority.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 12 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or 16 in certain European jurisdictions). If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us for immediate deletion.
